Let's Get Funky

Nothing says summer to me like funky pickles made with cucumbers from our garden.

Whenever someone asks for advice about how to make the best pickles, I tell them:

"You gotta wait a long time. The longer you wait the funkier they get." - Me

Tips & Tricks

  • Keep everything submerged under the brine to prevent mold
  • Fermentation time varies with temperature - warmer rooms ferment faster

The Recipe

Ingredient Quantity
Cucumbers 2 pounds
Filtered water 4 cups
Sea salt 3 tablespoons
Dill 4-5 large
Garlic 6-8 cloves
Black peppercorns 1 tablespoon
Bay leaves 2-3 leaves

Instructions

  1. Wash cucumbers
  2. Dissolve sea salt in filtered water to make a brine
  3. Place garlic, dill, and spices in the bottom of a clean mason jar
  4. Pack cucumbers tightly into the jar. Leaving 2 inches of headspace
  5. Pour brine over cucumbers, ensuring they are completely submerged
  6. Cover jar with a fermentation lid
  7. Leave the jar sitting on the counter away from direct sunlight
  8. After 3-7 days, taste test for desired sourness
  9. When they're ready, move to refrigerator to slow fermentation

Preparation time: 30 minutes active, 3-7 days fermentation

Servings: Makes 1 quart jar (about 15-20 pickles)
